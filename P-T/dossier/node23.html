Research Programme

Currently, there is an overwhelming trend towards integrating microcomputers into mechanical and electronic devices of daily use. Many of these embedded systems are distributed, reactive and safety-critical e.g. automotive and avionics applications. My current research aims to build embedded systems with predictable functionality and timing behavior by employing formal mathematical techniques at different stages of the design life cycle: system requirements, intermediate representations and system implementation.
