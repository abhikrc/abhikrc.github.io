Participation in Pedagogical Discussions

Recently, I participated in the Formal Methods Education Workshop at Toronto. The aim was to discuss with fellow educators the issues in teaching formal methods and logic to Computer Science students. In future, I plan to spend more energies in this direction. I am keen to try out mechanisms for highlighting the value of formal methods (in system design) to Computer Science students.
