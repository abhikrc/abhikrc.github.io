Designing new modules

I have proposed and designed one new graduate module called Automated Software Validation which covers different verification methods (model checking, theorem proving) for software validation. In addition, I have also designed the entire course contents of an undergraduate module (Critical Systems and their Verification) which part of the Computer Engineering programme at School of Computing. Module folders for both of these courses are included in my Teaching Portfolio.
