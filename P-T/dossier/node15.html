Use of novel software tools in teaching

I have used software tools developed in my research for teaching undergraduate courses. The Chronos software performance estimation tool was developed as part of my research in 2002 - 2004. I am currently using it for laboratory assignments in the undergraduate course Hardware Software Co-design, an elective in our Computer Engineering programme.
