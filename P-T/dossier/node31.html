Publications

My work has been published in prestigious journals and conferences such as ACM Transactions on Programming Languages and Systems (TOPLAS), Real-time Systems Journal, ACM/IEEE International Conference on Software Engineering (ICSE), Intl. Symposium on Formal Methods (FM), Intl. Conf. on Computer Aided Verification (CAV), ACM Design Automation Conference (DAC) and IEEE Real-time Systems Symposium (RTSS). We note here, that conferences form the primary publication venues in Computer Science; they are peer-reviewed, highly regarded and extremely competitive (with only 10-20% of papers accepted).

A full list of my publications is available online. The publication list also appears in my detailed CV along with the acceptance rates (ratio of paper submissions to accepted papers) for the conference papers.
