Service to International Academic Community

I have started a new workshop focussing on software validation, the International Workshop on Software Verification and Validation. It has been held for four consecutive years. In 2006, it was co-located with the Federated Logic Conferences (FLoC) at Seattle. I have also served as a program committee member of a number of conferences, and a reviewer for many journals/conferences. Details of these services are listed in the Documents on Research chapter (see here ) and  hence these are not repeated here.
