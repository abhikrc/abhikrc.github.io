Midterm Examination CS4271, 2004-05

 

  1. The midterm examination of CS 4271 (Critical Systems and their Verification) in 2004-05 is attached. In all my offerings of the course CS4271, the final examination has accounted for 20-25% of the grade.

 

  1. The questions test the students’ ability to apply their knowledge in realistic settings. For example, question A in page 2 of the attached midterm paper tests the students’ ability to apply their knowledge of system modeling in a protocol they could not have seen in the textbook.

 

  1. In this course, we discuss modeling and reasoning about concurrent systems which the students initially find a bit hard. In the midterm, we have questions (see question C, page 4 of the attached midterm exam) which test the students understanding of the concurrency/communication models studied in class. In the final examination, we go one step further. We test whether the students’ understanding of the concurrency models is in-depth enough to construct new models of concurrency/communication (e.g. see question D.1 in page 11 of the sample final exam question paper in this module folder).
