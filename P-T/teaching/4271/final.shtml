Final Examination  CS4271, 2004-05

  1. The final examination of CS 4271 (Critical Systems and their Verification) in 2004-05 is attached. In this year, the final examination accounted for 45% of the grade. In all my offerings of the course CS4271, the final examination has accounted for 40-50% of the grade.

 

  1. The questions require in-depth understanding of the topics covered in class. More importantly, they require the students’ ability to apply this understanding to real-life situations. An example of this can be seen from question B.1 (page 5 of the question paper) which requires the students to model a traffic light controller with various constraints, using the formal models studied in class

  2. Moreover, the examination tests not just the students’ ability to understand/apply the concepts studied in class. Rather it also tests whether the students can build on these to construct new concepts. An example of this kind of question is  question D.1 (page 11 of the question paper). In class, we had studied some models of concurrency and communication. In this question, the students have to use this understanding to build a new kind of process communication described in the question
