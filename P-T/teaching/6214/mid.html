Sample CS 6214 Midterm Examination

  • This is the midterm examination for CS 6214 in 2004-05 (last year). The solutions for each question are provided after the question. The midterm examination accounted for 20\% of the grade in 2004-05.

 

  • Some of the questions asked in past years' examinations are now given as class exercises, for example see question B.4 (page 4) which was given as class exercise in this year's lecture on Temporal Logics. The (pattern of) questions in the examination differs from year to year.

 

  • Some of the questions test the students' fundamental understanding of the topics. An example of such a question is B.1 (page 3). Other questions test a mix of technical understanding and overall problem solving skills.
