Sample CS 6214 Final Examination

  • This is the final examination for CS 6214 in 2004-05 (last year). The solutions for each question are provided after the question. The final examination accounted for 45\% of the grade in 2004-05.

 

  • Some of the questions asked in past years' examinations are now given as class exercises, for example see question A.3 (page 4) which was given as class exercise in this year's lecture on Temporal Logics. The (pattern of) questions in the examination differs from year to year.

 

  •  Some of the questions test the students' fundamental understanding of the topics. An example of such a question is A.1 (page 2). Other questions test a mix of technical understanding and overall problem solving skills.

 

  • The questions do not only test understanding and problem-solving skills. They test the student's ability to transport classroom knowledge to a different situation (e.g. see Question E, page 11).
