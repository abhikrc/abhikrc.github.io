CS 6214 Sample Term Project

  • The following is a sample term project report submitted by a student in the 2004-05 offering of CS 6214. The project was done on an individual basis (i.e. by one student).

 

  • The student made use of the PVS theorem proving tool which was discussed during the course. However, he did acquire more detailed knowledge on these techniques (beyond the lectures)  since they are relevant to his research as well. In other words, he used the course project to further his research goals.

 

  • Apart from the attached report (and a class presentation), each student was required to submit his/her programs (in this case the programming was done on top of a theorem prover) which went through review.

 

  • The report submitted by the student contained many detailed appendices which are not relevant for the purposes of these module folder, and they have been removed.
