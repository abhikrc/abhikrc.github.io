My family migrated from East Bengal to West Bengal in 1915, around hundred years ago. My grandfather moved to Calcutta where he completed his education. He was a professor of Anthropology in the University of Calcutta from where he retired after training an entire generation of academics. He was awarded the President of India Gold Medal for lifelong contributions in his field (Anthropology).

My parents have spent their lives in Calcutta, an electrical engineer and a schoolteacher by profession. In summary, my family has been in the City of Joy (as Calcutta is fondly referred to) ever since 1910, until I moved out for my doctoral studies to NY. After my doctoral studies, I moved to Singapore along with my spouse Tulika. Our son Jishnu was born in Singapore (2003).

The above picture was taken during a trip in 2011. Jishnu was 8 years old at that time.
